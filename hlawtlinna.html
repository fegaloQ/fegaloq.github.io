THUPUI Hei vawinah kan hotute duhsakna azarah thusawina hun tha tak min pek avangin ka lawm em em a. Amaherawhchu chanvo chanloh erawh hi chu ka duh khawp mai ah, kan thalai hruaituten min rawn biak lai pawn, midang zawng turin ka lo ngen ve hram hram ah. A chhan chu rawn inkhawm regular ber te kan ni lo chiang sia, kei aia inkhawm tha, sawi tur awm ang tha hi an awmin ka hria a, inkhawm hun kan nei tam siloa, chanvo hi chang zawk se ka ti em em a, an phu zawk viau avangin, mahse chuti chung chung min ruat ve erawh chu ka lawm a, Pathian leh kan hotute chungah lawmthu ka sawi hmasa ni sela. Chuan, chumi ka sawi lan takah chuan, hmani chu kan thiannu pakhat hian, ‘Fela chu, i duh duh danin Pathian rawng i bawl ah’ min tia …… Ka ngaihtuah ta, ani khawp mai a tiin. Sawi ang khan inkhawm te pawh ka duh dan leh ka remchan dan angin ka rawn kal mai ah, a nihna takah phei chuan kum 1 zet chu hetah ka awm tih pawh tuman an hrelo ni, kawng dang chi hrang hrangah in hman theih dan thuah pawh ka duh dan chen te chiah zelin ka ti a ni deuh ber ah. Ka hnial lo viau a ni. Amaherawhchu, ka ngaihtuah chhunzawm ta, midangte duh dan dana Pathian rawng bawl ai chuan ka va tha zawk tho mai awm mange ka tia. …. aaa mahni inchhan leh thiamthu sawi te hi chu, kan pianpui hlawm vek chu nih hi maw. …. Midangte hmuh atana inkhawm emaw, midangten, kohhran eng committee emaw ber member a tel te hi an lawm avanga tel emaw te ai chuan, kei, ti ve khanglang ta ila, mahni leh khawtlangte hmasawnna tur ngaihtuah reng reng a buai ve, theih ang tawk a, mahni awmhmunah, Pathian thu chhiar emaw, ngaithlak emaw, tawngtai ve hi a va mawi zawk ve thei mai awm mange o ka tia. Hei startup neih ve te kan rilruk chhoh avangin mahni tawk hian rilru hi a vak kual ve nasa em em mai ah, hun pawh in duh thawhna te a na ve thin a, awmze nei deuhin hun kan hmang reng bik bawk silo nen, chhuah tlem hrim hrim kha a tan nan kan thupui ber mai kha nia. Eng a pawh chu ni se, heng pahnih, mahni duh dan dana Pathian rawngbawl leh Midangte duh dan dana Pathian rawngbawl te hi chu, eng zawk zawk pawh hi a pawimawh ber hranpa lo khawp mai. A pawimawh leh tha ta ber zawk chu Pathian duh dan dana Pathian rawngbawl ho hi a ni ta ani. Tah chiah chuan zawhna lian tak kan chhan a ngai ta a.
Eve Jackson
