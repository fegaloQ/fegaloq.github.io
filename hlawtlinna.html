PATHIAN rawngbawl dan pathumte:

1. Mahni duh dan dan

2. Midangte duh dan dan

3. Pathian duh dan

 Hei vawinah kan hotute duhsakna azarah thusawina hun tha tak min pek avangin ka lawm em em a. Amaherawhchu chanvo chanloh erawh hi chu ka duh khawp mai ah, kan thalai hruaituten min rawn biak lai pawn, midang zawng turin ka lo ngen ve hram hram ah.

A chhan chu rawn inkhawm regular ber te kan ni lo chiang sia, kei aia inkhawm tha, sawi tur awm ang tha hi an awmin ka hria a, inkhawm hun kan nei tam siloa, chanvo hi chang zawk se ka ti em em a, an phu zawk viau avangin, mahse chuti chung chung min ruat ve erawh chu ka lawm a, Pathian leh kan hotute chungah lawmthu ka sawi hmasa ni sela.

Chuan, chumi ka sawi lan takah chuan, hmani chu kan thiannu pakhat hian, ‘Nag chu, i duh duh danin Pathian rawng i bawl ah’ min tia …… Ka ngaihtuah ta, ani khawp mai a tiin. Sawi ang khan inkhawm te pawh ka duh dan leh ka remchan dan angin ka rawn kal mai ah, a nihna takah phei chuan kum 1 zet chu hetah ka awm tih pawh tuman an hrelo ni, kawng dang chi hrang hrangah in hman theih dan thuah pawh ka duh dan chen te chiah zelin ka ti a ni deuh ber ah. Ka hnial lo viau a ni.

Amaherawhchu, ka ngaihtuah chhunzawm ta, midangte duh dan dana Pathian rawng bawl ai chuan ka va tha zawk tho mai awm mange ka tia. …. aaa mahni inchhan leh thiamthu sawi te hi chu, kan pianpui hlawm vek chu nih hi maw.

Midangte hmuh atana inkhawm emaw, midangten, kohhran eng committee emaw ber member a tel te hi an lawm avanga tel emaw te ai chuan, kei, ti ve khanglang ta ila, mahni leh khawtlangte hmasawnna tur ngaihtuah reng reng a buai ve, theih ang tawk a, mahni awmhmunah, Pathian thu chhiar emaw, ngaithlak emaw, tawngtai ve hi a va mawi zawk ve thei mai awm mange o ka tia.

Hei startup neih ve te kan rilruk chhoh avangin mahni tawk hian rilru hi a vak kual ve nasa em em mai ah, hun pawh in duh thawhna te a na ve thin a, awmze nei deuhin hun kan hmang reng bik bawk silo nen, chhuah tlem hrim hrim kha a tan nan kan thupui ber mai kha nia.

Eng a pawh chu ni se, heng pahnih, mahni duh dan dana Pathian rawngbawl leh Midangte duh dan dana Pathian rawngbawl te hi chu, eng zawk zawk pawh hi a pawimawh ber hranpa lo khawp mai.

A pawimawh leh tha ta ber zawk chu Pathian duh dan dana Pathian rawngbawl ho hi a ni ta ani. Tah chiah chuan zawhna lian tak kan chhan a ngai ta a.

2 Timothea 3: 14 - 17

Enge Pathian duh dan dan chu ni ta ang le? Kohhrana in hman hi mi Pathian lawm. Mite hmuha thil tha tih hi mi Pathian lawm, inkhawm kher hi mi Pathian lawm. A kawng chu anni teh meuh mai, mahse a farisai deuh em. Mahni chauha Pathian nena tawngtaina leh a thu thianghlim chhiar atanga in pawlna thuk tak nei, inkhawm ngai hranpa lo, a ni tin eizawna a Kristiana hriat em em chu Pathian chuan a lawm zawk teh meuh mai dawn sia, tih te ka rilru a lang chho viau viauah. Anni pawhin anamahni hmangaihtute chu an hmangaih thin asin 36: In Pain mi a khawngaih thin ang bawkin nangni pawhin mi khawngaiah thin rawh u Hmani kan retreat neiah khan kan speaker, Sailova, khan Khawngaihna leh Hmangaihna ah khan min luh thuk pui khawp maiah, kan chiang uar uar rengah. Chumi kan chian viau takah chuan enge kan tih let ve tur leh, tih kan in zawh chuan, Pathian hmangaih let a ni deuh pawp mai awm e. Pathian kan hmangaih let chuan enge kan tih tur le, a duh dan dan a awm chu a va ni ber ta mai em. A duh dan dan awm kuite hi chu tumah tak takin kan theiloa, a har a ni, chuvang reng a a khawngaihna dawng pawh kan nia. Amaherawhchu chumi kawngah chuan engtin nge kan chak leh zuala, hma kan sawn theih ang tih hi vawiin kan ngaihtuah ho tur chu ni. Khawngaihna leh hmangaihna hi, tunhnai kan retreat pahnih, a hmasa zawk rawngbawltu retreat, kan speaker te pahnih hian an rawn ngai pawimawh em em mai ah, Rev Lalhmingchhuanga Zongte khanin, kan rinna neih chhan kha a sawi chiang khawp mai ah, Pathianin min hmangaih leh khawngaih em em avangin kan tan a rawn in pe a, chumi avang ngawr ngawr chuan atan hian ka inpe letin, Kristian kan nih chhan a ni tiin. Sailovan, Khawngaihna atanga khawngaih leh chhawnna an rawn tlangau pui ve leh a, Pathianin malsawmna min vur nasat sia te sawiin. A pawimawh ngiat a niang, kan Pathian miten an thupui hlur avangin, chuvang chuan Pathian duh dan dan kan awm theihna tur, hmalak dan tur kan sawi ang.
